Mexican Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1/2 onion - diced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
    • 1 can diced tomatoes

Directions

  1. Add butter and rice to skillet over medium heat. Cook for 3-5 minutes or until starting to brown
  2. Added onion, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. Cook until onions begin to soften.
  3. Add tomato paste and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add chicken broth and diced tomatoes. Heat to a simmmer. Then reduce heat ot low and cover. Cook for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Fluff with a fork and enjoy!
