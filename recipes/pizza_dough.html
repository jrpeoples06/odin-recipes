Pizza Dough


Home

Neapolitan-style pizza is an amazing dough to learn how to make. Its silky smooth texture creates an easy-to-shape dough that yields a wonderfully chewy, crispy crust. This is destined to be your new go-to recipe for pizza night!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (232g) King Arthur '00' Pizza Flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon instant yeast or active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons (8g) table salt
  • 3/4 cup (170g) water, lukewarm, (105F to 115F)
