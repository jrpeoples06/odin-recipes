Hashbrown Omelette

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • Frozen hashbrowns
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • 2 eggs - beaten
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream

Directions

  1. heat 3 tablespoons of cooking oil over medium high heat.
  2. Add hashbrowns and cook for 7 minutes.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon cooking oil. Add salt, black pepper, and garlic powder to taste.
  4. Flip and cook for 4 minutes
  5. Add 2 beaten eggs. cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Flip and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Add desired ammount of shredded cheese. Fold omelette in half.
  8. Plate and add desipred ammount of sour cream.
