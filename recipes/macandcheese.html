Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 1/2 cups macoroni noodles
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup parmesean cheese

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil over medium-high heat and add garlic. Cook for 2 mintues
  2. Add chicken broth, macoroni noodles, salt, pepper and paprika. Heat until almost a boil.
  3. Turn the heat down to medium-low. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir and cover. Reduce heat to low. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add cheddar cheese, cream cheese, and butter. Turn off heat and cover for 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in milk and parmeseam cheese.
